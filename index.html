Created by Marvel Comic writers Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, Spider-Man first graced the pages of American comic books in August 1962, making his appearance in Amazing Fantasy #15. During the age where teenage and child comic book characters were relegated to sidekick or other supporting roles, Spider-Man was the first of his kind, dealing with everyday struggles adolescent readers could relate to. (Well, minus the whole spider bite from a radioactive spider turning him into a superhero thing.)
